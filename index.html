Instructions

  1. Click on the image you want to write about.
  2. You will see a list of words describing that image which have been suggested by the AI. You can use some or all of these in your writing.
  3. Type into the black box. As you type, the system will suggest additional words for you to use in the table below, just click on one to include it.
  4. When you are happy with your writing, click on the PUBLISH link (on the right, below the writing area)
  5. Give your writing a title if you like
  6. Click SUBMIT. You will see a link e.g. http://botnik.org/read?id=VXrO.
  7. If you would like a postcard of your story then write this link into the paper form on the desk (it's case sensitive!), along with your details
